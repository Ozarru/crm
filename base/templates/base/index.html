Welcome to the CRM

A Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system is a powerful tool designed to help businesses manage and analyze customer interactions and data throughout the customer lifecycle. The primary goal of a CRM is to improve customer relationships, enhance customer satisfaction, and drive sales growth.

Home

The home page serves as the central hub of your web app, providing users with an overview of the key features and functionalities available. It often includes a dashboard with quick access to important metrics, recent activities, notifications, and links to other sections of the app.

Categories

The categories section allows users to organize products into distinct groups for easier navigation and management. Users can create, edit, and delete categories, making it simple to classify products based on their attributes.

Products

The products section displays a comprehensive list of items available for sale. Users can add new products, edit existing ones, or remove products that are no longer available.

Point of sale

The point of sale (POS) feature enables users to process transactions and manage sales in real-time. This section usually includes a user-friendly interface for selecting products, entering quantities, applying discounts, and completing purchases.

Sales

The sales section provides an overview of all completed transactions, including details such as date, time, total amount, and customer information. Users can filter, search, and analyze sales data to identify trends, track performance, and generate reports.

Invoicing

The invoicing section allows users to create, manage, and send invoices to clients. Users can generate invoices based on sales transactions, customize invoice templates, and track payment statuses. This feature helps streamline the billing process and maintain accurate financial records.

Clients

The clients section is dedicated to managing customer information. Users can add new clients, update existing profiles, and view detailed information such as contact details, purchase history, and outstanding invoices. This section helps foster strong client relationships and improve customer service.

Users

The users section manages user accounts and permissions within the web app. Admins can create new user profiles, assign roles (e.g., admin, staff, sales), and set access levels to various features. This ensures secure and organized management of the app while facilitating collaboration among team members.
